Who Am I?

I’m a BI Engineer at Amazon who bridges research science, data engineering and operations to drive supply‑chain innovation at scale. My mission is to transform raw data into actionable insights that deliver faster, smarter and more efficient outcomes. With a strong foundation in advanced analytics, machine learning, deep learning, NLP, Gen AI and operations research—and hands‑on expertise with AWS, Power BI, QuickSight and other cloud‑native tools—I deliver business impact across supply chain, marketing, operations and customer experience domains. Before Amazon, I honed my analytics skills in diverse roles at PwC, IBM and Punjab National Bank. Beyond my day job I’m a passionate career mentor and analytics evangelist whose community learning content has reached over 5,000 followers and whose blogs on analytics and cricket continue to inspire.
